Lake Wylie lawyer named Solo General Counsel of Year

March 21, 2016

Allen Nason of Lake Wylie has been recognized as the 2016 Solo General Counsel of the Year by Charlotte Business Journal and the Association of Corporate Counsel for his work with Eastwood Homes, the nation's 25th largest homebuilder.

"Being a general counsel satisfies the aspects of why I wanted to become a lawyer," Nason said. "I get to set strategy, execute it and see what the end result is."
