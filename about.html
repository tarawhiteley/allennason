About me.

Allen Nason is a former Army JAG, Assistant U.S. Attorney, and large law firm partner who has served as General Counsel to Eastwood Homes and its affiliates since 2007. As General Counsel, he has navigated the company’s complex and ever-changing legal landscape with unparalleled success, and is involved in all sentinel business affairs and decision making of the Company.

During his career, Mr. Nason has represented more than a dozen public or privately-held homebuilders, numerous developers, title insurers and homeowner’s associations on sentinel business and legal matters of every variety. He has directed several prior asset acquisition and merger and acquisition transactions in his career, and has substantial experience with bankruptcy workouts and §363 asset sales to facilitate Company growth.

Mr. Nason is licensed to practice law in North and South Carolina, as well as in Virginia, and is an honor graduate of the Seattle University School of Law. He also holds a Business Marketing Degree from Winthrop University, and was honored as Order of the Coif and received the Presidential Award of Excellence for leadership.

He was awarded 2016 solo General Counsel of the Year by the Charlotte Business Journal and the Association of Corporate Counsel.

Mr. Nason is married, has two children, and competes in numerous tennis leagues in the Charlotte, North Carolina area.
